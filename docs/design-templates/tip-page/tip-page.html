Peel Garlic in 10 Seconds Flat

schedule 2 mins

format_list_numbered Easy Steps

1

Smash the bulb

Place the whole garlic bulb on your counter and give it a firm smash with the heel of your hand to separate the cloves.

2

Bowl them up

Put all the cloves into one of the large metal or ceramic bowls. Discard the loose papery skin from the bulb.

3

Shake it like a polaroid picture

Invert the second bowl on top to create a dome. Hold them together tightly and shake vigorously for 10-15 seconds. Don't hold back!

4

Reveal the magic

Open the bowls. You should see perfectly peeled cloves separate from their skins. Pick out the cloves and you're ready to cook!

lightbulb

Why this works

The hard shaking causes the cloves to hit against the hard sides of the bowls and against each other. This impact, combined with the friction, bruises the skin enough to loosen it, while the centrifugal force helps fling the lightweight skins off the heavier cloves.
